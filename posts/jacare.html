Jacarè

← Back to all posts

The passage to Jacarè was the last leg we did with Mario as a crew. It was easier than the previous one but had its own challenges. Especially navigating in the river at night with no moon. Mario has usual was of great help.

Once in Jacarè, we left the boat in one of the only 3 marinas that there are (the only decent marinas north of Salvador, as we found out later). We wanted to spend a few days off the boat with Mario. So we took an Airbnb in Pipa, the world famous surfer destination.
